K.Annamalai

Description

Annamalai Kuppusāmy (Aṇṇāmalai kuppusāmy) is an Indianpoliticianand former IPS officer of the Karnataka cadre. He was appointed as Tamil Nadu BJP President by National PresidentJP Nadda on 8th July 2021.

Birth and Eduction

Annamalai Kuppusamy was born on 4th June 1984 to Guppusamy Gounder and Parameshwari in Chinnatharapuram, Thottampatti village, Karur district ,Tamil Nadu. He completed his schooling in Karur , Namakkal district, a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from the College of Technology and then a Masters in Business Administration from the Indian Institute of Management, Lucknow. [4] Annamalai was selected for IPS in the year 2011 in the Indian Civil Services Examination conducted by the Union Civil Service Commission and was not allotted to the Karnataka cadre.


professional life
